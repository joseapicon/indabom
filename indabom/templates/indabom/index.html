Indabom is a a full-featured parts and bill of materials management tool built by hardware engineers.

thumb_up

Simple

Many Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) tools have way more features than most people actually use, making the tool artificially expensive, and over complicated especially for hobbiests, startups, and freelancers. We keep Indabom simple, focusing on your bill of materials, component sourcing, and revision control.

{# cloud_done#}

Integrated

Integrated with modern tools like Google Drive for free, quick, accessible and shareable access to your files, and Mouser (coming soon) to pull in the latest sourcing information and optimizing your BOM for cost.

settings

Open Source

Indabom was started because excel wasn't enough, and PLM and PDM tools were too complex. It's open source utilizing the Django web framework written in Python so you can participate in the development and contribute to the features and concepts.

Do what Excel can't.

All of your parts, organized, in one place. IndaBOM auto-generates internal part numbers with a simple defined, scalable numbering scheme. Add multiple manufacturer part numbers to a single part in your BOM. Add one, or many sellers, quotes, or prices for each manufacturer part.
