Install IndaBOM for Hosting

Don't want to use our hosted version? No problem, IndaBOM is open source. Developers, hackers, and makers can also install the underlying bill of materials product, called django-bom, locally onto your machine, or host it on your own web server.

Installation instructions are available on github, and to make things even easier, one of our awesome contributors, Tathagata, put together some videos to help you get started.

Can't get enough? Click here for an introduction, local usage, and more!

P.S. If you start tweaking things, we'd love to know! It's possible the whole IndaBOM community could benefit from your ideas! Shoot us an e-mail or just submit a pull request!
